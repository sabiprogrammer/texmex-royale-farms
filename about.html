About Us

By creating a financial link between farm-investors and farmers, Tex-Mex Royale Farms (incorporated as Tex-Mex Royale Int'l, RC 1873273) is well-positioned to turn Nigeria into the region that feeds the country and beyond. It also equips enthusiastic young people with the knowledge and abilities needed to create sustainable agribusinesses.

Tex-Mex Royale is situated in Nigeria's Ondo State, Akure. We are completely engaged in the supply of agricultural inputs, chemicals, and fertilizers. We are heavily involved in crop production, fishing, poultry, piggery, food processing, irrigation installation, agricultural consultancy, marketing, promotion, export of high-quality farm products, and other farm setup contracts. We also provide agricultural engineering services.


Vision

To dominate Nigeria's agricultural products distribution chain and training industries by 2030.


Mission

To use new technology to promote sustainable agriculture and turn Nigeria into the global food powerhouse.


Core Values

Passion, Honesty, Fidelity and Service.
