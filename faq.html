What is Tex-mex Royale Farms?

Tex-Mex Royale Farms is a Nigerian based agro-allied business with headquarters in Akure, Ondo State. We specialize in agricultural enterprises, including the provision of pesticides, fertilizers, and other agro inputs. We are heavily involved in crop production, fishing, poultry, piggery, food processing, irrigation installation, agricultural consultancy, marketing, promotion, and export of high-quality farm products. We also provide agricultural engineering services.

What issues are we addressing?

Numerous issues face Nigeria's agricultural sector, but the lack of farm financing and market access for traceable goods rank at the top. Bringing together various interest groups whose interests are aligned, from farm investors who want some return on investment to active professional farmers who are seeking and knowledgeable in mass cultivation, harvesting and reproduction. Our future-focused technology platform, Tex-Mex Royale, is positioned to transform Nigeria into the hub that feeds the world by matching active farm activities with the finance and skills to practice sustainable agriculture.

What is the process?

Tex-Mex Royale offers a pre-selected list of farms with a variety of products and animals that have been carefully picked based on demand, yield, Return on Investment (ROI), and investment tenure. These are broken down into smaller portions that correspond to each farm's required minimum investment. We gather investments from agricultural lovers who have money to invest in the agricultural industry at Tex-Mex Royale. These funds are employed in various value chains to finance agricultural clusters. A potential farm investor would use our site to browse all the farms with their matching ROI, Expected Yield, and Tenure of Investment shown, read the FAQs and ask questions. They would then register and choose to finance the farm of their choosing. The farm investor can decide to arrange onsite farm visits if he so desires. The Farm-Investor receives his initial investment returned after the investment period, together with returns that vary depending on the crop and the length of the investment.

Who is eligible to invest?

The investment procedure is completed online through our website, and both individuals and business entities, Nigerians residing in Nigeria or the Diaspora, as well as non-Nigerians, are welcome to participate. If you are passionate about the Agricultural Industry or have some investable cash, it makes no difference whether you are employed or self-employed.

How much can I invest?

You can invest as much as you can in the chosen farm project because each of our farm projects has a cost-effective pricing structure. You can make investments in two or more farms, whether they are part of the same farm or other farms. Explore our investment packages (hyperlink) to learn more about how they are set up.

What do I gain as an investor?

The agricultural project you invest in will determine how much money you make. This is so because the Return on Investment (ROI), Contract Period, and Investment Cost for various farm projects vary. The investor receives his money back plus a return on investment (ROI) appropriate to the agricultural project he invested in. Visit our investment packages (hyperlink) to learn more.

What is the required minimum investment?

Our agricultural initiatives each have a required minimum investment. Visit our investment packages (hyperlink) to find the required minimum for each agricultural project.

What is the project's management style?

On behalf of all the investors, Tex-Mex Royale oversees the various farm projects and makes sure that best practices are followed for maximum output.

How do we generate revenue?

When you make money, so do we! After our investors and farmers have gotten their cut, we split the remaining portion of the earnings.

What if the crops fail to produce?

All agricultural projects would be insured to ensure the security of each investor's cash.

Can foreign investors invest?

Everyone is welcome to invest, from any nation on the globe. Our website makes it simple to make an online payment for the chosen agricultural project.

How can I receive my money back once the investment period has ended?

Both the initial investment and the ROI are duly sent to the Farm investor's provided account information
